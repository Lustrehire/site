Ultimate Candle Burn-Time Calculator for Evening Ceremonies

August 3, 2025

Whether you're planning a twilight wedding ceremony or a candlelit reception, one of the most overlooked details is ensuring your candles last the entire evening. Here's the ultimate burn-time guide for sand wax candles to help you plan confidently.

How Long Do Sand Wax Candles Last?

At Lustre Hire, our pre-filled sand wax candles are designed to give you maximum glow with minimal stress:

    Each standard fill burns for approximately 6 to 8 hours depending on wind exposure and placement

    Re-lightable for use over two consecutive nights if needed

Ideal Candle Quantities by Event Duration

Use this quick-reference table to estimate what you’ll need based on how long your event will run:

Event Length Start Time End Time Recommended Candle Use
2 hours 6:00 PM 8:00 PM 1 fill (no issues)
4 hours 5:00 PM 9:00 PM 1 fill (ideal range)
6 hours 4:30 PM 10:30 PM 1 fill (monitor wind)
8+ hours 4:00 PM Midnight Use reserve candles or prep for relight

Tips to Maximise Burn Time

    Shield candles from wind using floral arrangements or hurricane glass sleeves

    Spread candles evenly across your space to avoid burn hotspots

    Light candles just before guests arrive to extend the glow
