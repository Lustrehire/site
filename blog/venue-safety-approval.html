Venue Safety 101: Getting Candle Approval from Popular Perth Reception Spots

August 3, 2025

Planning a candlelit wedding in Perth? Venue regulations on candle use can vary widely, often requiring couples to seek formal approval. Here’s your practical guide to securing venue approval smoothly and confidently.

Why Venues Have Candle Restrictions

Reception venues prioritise safety to protect their spaces and guests. Typical concerns include:

Fire hazards
Wax spills
Smoke and soot damage

Fortunately, sand wax candles from Lustre Hire specifically address these concerns.

Why Sand Wax Candles are Venue-Approved

Enclosed Flame: Our sand wax candles are safely contained within clear glass cylinders, significantly reducing the risk of open flames.

Drip-Free & Mess-Free: The finely textured sand wax remains cleanly within the cylinder, preventing wax drips and spills.

Smoke-Free: Sand wax candles burn cleanly, minimising smoke and soot.

How to Gain Venue Approval

Follow these straightforward steps to get candle approval from your chosen venue:

1. Contact Your Venue Early: Reach out to your venue coordinator well in advance.

2. Provide Clear Information: Clearly outline the safety features of Lustre Hire’s sand wax candles.

3. Demonstrate Compliance: Highlight how sand wax candles adhere to venue safety standards.
