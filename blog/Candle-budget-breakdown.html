Breakdown of a $500 Candle Budget: 3 Looks, 3 Price Points

August 3, 2025

Planning a candlelit wedding or event on a $500 budget? Here’s exactly what you can achieve with Lustre Hire’s signature sand wax candles. Whether you're styling a sweetheart table or glowing up your entire reception, we’ll show you three looks at three price points - all within budget.

Why Budgeting by Look Matters

Candles are one of the simplest ways to elevate your styling, but if you’re planning everything yourself, you want clarity on what your money gets you.

Lustre Hire pricing is simple and scales by quantity:

The first 44 candles are $10 each

Candles 45 to 89 are $9 each

Candles beyond 89 are $8 each

That means you can mix and match candle sizes and still get the same flat rate - no guesswork.

Look 1: Intimate Glow

Budget Used: $150

Candles Included: 15 candles at $10 each

Leftover Budget: $350 for flowers, signage or other styling pieces

Perfect for:

Elopement dinners

Sweetheart tables

Lounge area styling

Suggested Layout:

Three candle clusters with 10 candles each

Mix 10cm, 15cm and 30cm heights for depth

Style with a soft table runner or bud vases

Look 2: Celebration Glow

Budget Used: $449

Candles Included: 45 candles with the first 44 at $10 each and the 45th at $9

Leftover Budget: $51 for extras like fresh greenery or printed menus

Perfect for:

Small wedding receptions

Aisle glow and tables combined

Long table or bar styling

Suggested Layout:

Six round guest tables with 6 to 7 candles each

Candle trio for bar or drinks station

Extra candles for the ceremony aisle or welcome table

Look 3: Grand Glow

Budget Used: $845

Candles Included: 90 candles with tiered pricing applied

Leftover Budget: Not applicable - this setup goes beyond a $500 budget for maximum glow

Perfect for:

Full candle coverage across your venue

Ceremony to reception transitions

Statement installations without overspending

9 guest tables with 8 to 10 candles each

Feature area like sweetheart table or ceremony backdrop with 12 to 15 candles
