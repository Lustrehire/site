Planning a 30-Candle Tablescape for under $450: Here’s the Checklist

August 3, 2025

Creating a stunning candlelit tablescape doesn’t have to break the bank. With careful planning and smart choices, you can design an elegant 30-candle tablescape for under $450. Here’s your detailed step-by-step checklist to guide you through the process.

Step-by-Step Tablescape Checklist

1. Choose Your Candles ($300 - Lustre Hire’s Intimate Glow Package)

Select 30 ready-to-light sand wax candles from Lustre Hire (Intimate Glow Package at $10 each). Confirm candle availability and secure your booking early.

2. Decide on Linens and Runners ($60 or less)

Opt for neutral linen or natural burlap runners to complement the candlelit aesthetic. Check local rental services or affordable homeware stores for best prices.

3. Incorporate Floral Elements ($60 or DIY from garden)

Purchase budget-friendly greenery and small florals like eucalyptus or baby’s breath. Alternatively, forage from gardens or nature responsibly.

4. Select Dinnerware and Glassware (Use Existing or Rent)

Utilize existing dinnerware and glassware or consider affordable rental options. Keep the palette neutral to accentuate the candlelight.

5. Add Decorative Accents ($30)

Incorporate simple decorative items such as place cards, small votives, or textured napkin rings. DIY or source from affordable retailers or online marketplaces.
