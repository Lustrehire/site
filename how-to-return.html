How to Return Your Candles

Returning your order is simple

After your event, simply repack your candles carefully and return them in the original packaging at your agreed return time. We’ll take care of the cleaning, so you only need to focus on packing everything back safely.

Packed in foam inserts
Designed for secure handling
We handle the cleaning

The return process

Follow these same steps after every event to keep everything safe, tidy, and ready for return.

Step 1

Blow out candles

Once your event is finished, make sure all candles are fully extinguished before packing them away.

Step 2

Let wax cool

Allow the wax to cool before moving or packing the candles.

Step 3

Keep upright

Please keep the candles upright while repacking and transporting them to help avoid spills or shifting.

Step 4

Repack them

Place each candle back into its original foam insert or crate so everything is secure for transport.

Step 5

Return them

Bring your order back at the agreed return time window after your event.

Good news

We handle the cleaning, so there’s no need to clean the candles before returning them. Just repack everything safely in the original packaging and bring it back at your agreed time.

Secure from pickup to return

Our candles are packed in foam inserts for secure handling, so the return process is usually a simple lift-out, lift-back-in system.

Original packaging matters Each candle should go back into its foam insert or crate for transport.
Simple in and out process Most returns are quick because everything is already packed to travel safely.
No pre-cleaning needed Just repack carefully and return at your agreed time. We handle the cleaning.

Damage or loss

If any items are not returned or are seriously damaged, this may affect your refundable bond. In normal hire situations, it is returned in full after your candles are returned and checked.

Need help?

If you’re unsure about anything, please get in touch before returning your order and we’ll be happy to help.
