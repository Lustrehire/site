Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes are available?

We offer Large (10x30 cm), Medium (10x15 cm), and Small (10x10 cm) sizes. All are ready-to-light, pillar-style sand wax candles designed for a beautiful, layered effect.

How does the rental period work?

Our rental period is a daily hire. However, you can pick up the candles the day before your event and return them the day after (by 5 PM) at no extra cost.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellations are allowed, but please note that your deposit is non-refundable. This ensures we can reserve your candles for your event.

Do you offer delivery?

We currently offer in-store pickup only. All rentals are pick-up and drop-off from our Perth location to keep costs low for our customers.

What if I accidentally damage a candle?

If a candle or glass sleeve is damaged during your hire, the cost of replacement will be deducted from your deposit. Our candles are durable, but we recommend handling them with care.

How do I make a booking?

You can make a booking by contacting us directly. Once confirmed, you will need to pay a deposit to secure your candles. The remaining balance is due at pick-up.
