How It Works

A simple DIY hire process designed to keep things easy before, during, and after your event.

1. Check Your Date Enter your event date and candle quantity to check live availability and get your quote.
2. Book Online Complete your details, review your booking, and pay securely online to lock in your date.
3. Pick Up Collect your candles from our pickup address the day before your event during your selected pickup time window.
4. Style & Enjoy Set them out on the day and enjoy the candlelit look without the prep work. All candles come pre-filled and ready to light.
5. Return Blow them out, repack them in the original packaging, and return them the day after your event during your selected return time window. We handle the cleaning.
